Research Areas

🔗
Causal Neuro-Symbolic AI

Integrating causal knowledge into deep learning for explainability and robustness via the CausalNeSy framework — enabling models that reason like humans, not just pattern-match.

Learn more →
🕸️
Knowledge Graphs & Ontologies

Building structured representations for autonomous driving, industrial metaverse, and biomedical applications — CausalKG and MetaverseKG bridge symbolic knowledge with neural systems.

Learn more →
🏥
AI for Healthcare

Predicting pediatric asthma outcomes, modeling personalized treatment effects, and building transparent clinical decision support — AI that doctors and patients can trust.
