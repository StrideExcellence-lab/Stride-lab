Approach

Teaching Philosophy

Dr. Jaimini's teaching philosophy centers on making complex AI concepts accessible through real-world examples, hands-on experimentation, and critical thinking. She believes that the best way to understand AI is to build it — and to question it.

She fosters inclusive classrooms where diverse perspectives strengthen the learning experience, and actively mentors students from underrepresented backgrounds. Her goal is to produce researchers and practitioners who are both technically rigorous and ethically grounded.
