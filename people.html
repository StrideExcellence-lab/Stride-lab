Dr. Utkarshani Jaimini

Assistant Professor · Principal Investigator
Dept. of Computer & Information Science, University of Michigan – Dearborn

Dr. Jaimini is a full-time tenure-track Assistant Professor at the University of Michigan–Dearborn. She completed her PhD from the AI Institute, University of South Carolina, advised by Dr. Amit Sheth. Her research focuses on advancing Neuro-Symbolic AI by integrating causal reasoning to improve explainability, robustness, and human-like understanding in AI systems.

She has industry experience from Bosch Center for AI and Siemens, and has contributed to NIH and NSF grant proposal writing and multi-disciplinary collaborative projects.
